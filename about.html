by their side believes in a world of accountability, justice and safety for women and gender-diverse people.

our story

Our story began long before this project had a name.

If you grew up in our community, you’ve seen it too — the quiet ways harm shows up in our everyday lives. The jokes that aren’t really jokes. The way young men talk about women when they think no one is listening. The way young women learn, far too early, to shrink themselves or to excuse behaviour that chips away at their safety and self-worth.

Violence sits at every corner of our community, sometimes loud and obvious, sometimes soft and familiar. And for a lot of us, it became normal… until it didn’t. Until we started paying attention to the patterns. Until we realized just how many young people were falling into cycles of control, manipulation, and harm long before adulthood.

by their side was created to interrupt that cycle — with empathy, with conversation, and with the belief that our community deserves more than survival. We deserve care, accountability, and spaces where young people can talk honestly about their relationships, their identities, and the kind of love they hope to receive and give.

our vision

We imagine a community where young people have the tools, skills, and confidence to build relationships rooted in honesty, respect, and care — not fear.

A community where young women, gender-diverse people, and young men all sit at the table together, learning how to unlearn what harms us and relearn what heals us. Because preventing violence isn’t the work of one group — it’s a cultural shift, and we all have a role in shaping it.

Our vision is a world where young people get to grow in safety, kindness, joy, and love — supported not just to avoid harm, but to build relationships they’re proud of.
